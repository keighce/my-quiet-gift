On Ordinary Days

Gratitude in Every Thought 🌙

Every time I think of you, I feel a soft wave of gratitude.
For the kindness in your words, the moments we shared,
and even for the silence between us.
It's all been meaningful in its own way.
Grateful for every piece of you that's crossed my path.

Grateful for Quiet Moments 🌸

It's in the little, unspoken moments that I feel most thankful.
Like when I see something that reminds me of you, and for a second,
I can't help but smile.
It's not loud or demanding — just a quiet recognition that
I'm grateful for your presence in my thoughts, even from afar.

I Didn't Know Where to Put This 💫

So I placed it here.
Not in your inbox. Not on your feed.
But in a quiet, out-of-the-way place — where it wouldn't ask anything from you, except maybe a pause.
Some feelings don't fit into conversations, only corners.

Thankful for You, Even from a Distance 🌿

I don't need to see you to feel the warmth of your spirit.
I'm grateful for the quiet strength you've shown,
and for the way you've made my days better, even when you're not around.
It's a quiet kind of gratitude, one that doesn't ask for anything
but is still filled with all the good things you've given without even knowing it.

In the Small Things 🌱

I've learned to appreciate the little things — the unspoken kindness,
the quiet gestures that don't need recognition.
Like the way your presence lingers even when you're not near.
It's in these small moments, these quiet acts, that I find my deepest gratitude.

A Soft Thank You 🌺

Thank you for simply being,
for showing up as you are,
for the quiet strength and honesty you bring into my life.
This isn't a thank you that demands anything in return,
but just a soft acknowledgment that I see you,
and I'm grateful for everything you are.
