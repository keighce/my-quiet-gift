Dear, Scndalous Schottenstein

I've thought about writing this for some time — not because I expect anything from it, but because sometimes gratitude just needs a place to live. This is mine.

There's so much about you that I admire — not just in the obvious ways, but in the subtle strength you carry without drawing attention to it. The way you keep going, even when things are heavy. The kindness in your actions, the way you manage to be thoughtful without needing to be loud about it. I notice those things. And I'm proud of you, even from a distance.

You've handled so much with grace. You've stayed true to yourself in a world that often makes that difficult. Watching you grow, choose yourself, and keep showing up — it's something I deeply respect.
